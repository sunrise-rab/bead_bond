Health & Safety

We want every BeadBond session to be safe, calm, and enjoyable. Please read the guidance below before attending.

Supervision

  • Children must be supervised by a parent/guardian unless the session is a school-led booking.
  • Staff do not provide 1:1 care. Please inform us of any support needs in advance.

Small Parts & Choking Risk

  • Beads and accessories are small items and can be a choking hazard.
  • We recommend sessions for children aged 5+ (or younger only with very close adult supervision).

Allergies & Sensitivities

  • Please tell us about allergies (e.g., latex, metals, fragrance sensitivity) before the session.
  • We avoid harsh chemicals and use child-friendly materials where possible.

Hygiene

  • Hands should be washed or sanitised at the start of the activity.
  • Work surfaces and tools are cleaned between sessions.
  • Please do not attend if your child is unwell (fever, vomiting, contagious infection).

Behaviour & Respect

  • We encourage kindness, sharing, and calm behaviour.
  • Bullying, unsafe behaviour, or damage to equipment may result in the session being ended.

Photography

  • Photos are only taken if you give consent at booking.
  • You can withdraw consent at any time during the session.

Emergency & First Aid

  • Basic first-aid supplies are available.
  • In an emergency we will contact the parent/guardian and, if needed, emergency services.

Questions? Contact us via the Contact Us page.
